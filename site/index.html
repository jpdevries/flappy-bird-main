Welcome to Flippy Flappy!

Pressing space bar or clicking starts the game, and also controls Flappy's flight.

Help Flappy fly between the pipes without crashing, and earn a point each time Flappy successfully navigates a pipe set.

For an added bonus, try pressing the 'F' key to make Flappy perform a backflip. Each backflip adds a point to your score.

To pause/unpause the game, simply press the 'P' key.

Good luck!
